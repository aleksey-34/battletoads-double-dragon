FAQ

Common questions answered.

Is it really free? ▼
Yes. During our beta period, all core features are free. We'll introduce paid tiers later, but the free tier will always exist.
Can I lose money? ▼
All trading carries risk. Our strategies are backtested extensively, but past performance does not guarantee future results. Never trade with money you can't afford to lose.
Is my exchange account safe? ▼
We only use read-only API keys with trade permissions. We never have access to withdraw or transfer your funds. Your assets stay on your exchange.
Which exchanges are supported? ▼
We currently support Binance, Bybit, OKX, KuCoin, Gate.io, and HTX, with more exchanges being added regularly.
Do I need coding skills? ▼
Not at all. BTDD is designed for non-technical traders. Connect your exchange, pick a strategy, and activate. Everything is point-and-click.
